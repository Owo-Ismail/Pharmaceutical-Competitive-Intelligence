# Pharmaceutical Competitive Intelligence Dashboard

## Business Problem

The objective was to identify where Empa is winning or losing against Dapa at territory, brick, and sales representative levels.

The goal was to determine where sales resources should be invested to maximize future Empa growth.

## Stakeholders

- Sales Director
- Regional Sales Managers
- Brand Managers
- Commercial Excellence Team
- Sales Representatives

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Excel

## KPIs

- Growth KPIs
- Market Share KPIs
- Share Change KPIs
